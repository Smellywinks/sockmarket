What is Chalkboard?

Chalkboard allows streamlined communication between Supervisors and their Teams. Whereas other large systems require dedicated administrators and seem to make life harder, Chalkboard is simple.

Event Supervisors can simply Login with their Email and easily begin creating and editing company event pages. You will seamlessly be able to import client lists, set up meeting times, post announcements, and more -- all from a simple dashboard.
